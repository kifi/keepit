Our Mission
Kifi’s mission is to help connect people with knowledge. We are building a new kind of Knowledge Management system, one that helps you manage the knowledge that exists on the Web.

We spend a ton of time thinking about Knowledge Management more generally and how companies can use Web tools to help build and maintain their Knowledge bases. In a piece on Forbes.com, we recently wrote about how to keep your company’s knowledge from walking out the door when a key teammate leaves. There are two priorities to keep in mind: Keep a relationship with your co-worker even after he or she is gone, and make make sure the knowledge the teammates have doesn’t reside only in their heads.
