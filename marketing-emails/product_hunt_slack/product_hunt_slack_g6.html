Now you can set up a Slack sync with your Kifi team!
 
The full sync will let your Slack team members sign up to Kifi via Slack and you could very easily create a matching Kifi library for any slack channel.
 
 
We had just released this feature on Product Hunt, check out the notes and what other users are saying about it.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Slack to Kifi
 
Links you share in Slack channels are automatically kept. Your search results will take you back to the conversation, where the links were shared.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Full Text Search
 
Kifi creates a full-text index of all keeps. They are searchable from within Slack using the slash command /kifi [query] and on Google using our extension.
 
 
 
 
 
You get the most value by making the integration work both ways. You send the links you share in a Kifi library to your Slack channel, and make those links and their content searchable in Slack. You can also send all the links in your Slack channel to a Kifi Library, and make all the links in your Slack channel searchable within Kifi or Slack.
