  Kifi for Teams features include  
 
 
 
 
 
Easy Collaboration
 
Create a Library within a team, where you can put all of your web research. New team members get access to all team visible libraries; which is great for onboarding new employees.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
On-Page Chat
 
If you want to send a single web page, you can do that with the click of a button. You can also chat with your whole team at once, in context on the page.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Slack integration
 
Anything you save in a Kifi Library will be automatically channeled on Slack.
 
 
 
 
 
 
Artisan privacy settings
 
You can control whether your team libraries are public, visible to the team, or private, and which team members are collaborators.
