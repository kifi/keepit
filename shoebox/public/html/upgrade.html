How to update Kifi to version 2.0?

It’s easy as a pie:

  1. Go to the extension page at: chrome://chrome/extensions/ (or by typing it on your browser)
  2. At the Kifi extension, hit the “Reload” button.
  3. Make sure your current version is now version 2.0

All Done!
