Team Settings

Library settings

Who can create public libraries within your team?

Select who is able to make your teams' libraries public. Public libraries are discoverable in search engine's results, like Google.

Who can edit libraries within your team?

Select who is able to edit any library settings such as visibility, title, and membership.

Who can move libraries out of the team space?

Select who is able to move libraries out of the company and into another location ex. another team.

Member settings

Who can see the members page of this team?

Select who is able to see the team's members page. "Anyone" means it's also discoverable in other search engine's results like Google.

Who can invite members to join the team?

Members of the team have access to your team visible libraries. In most scenarios they can also keep to any public or team visible library.

Features

Who can message everyone in the team?

Send a same page chat to everyone in the team with just a couple of clicks.

Integrations

Who can create a slack integration with Kifi?

Send all of your keeps from a particular library, automatically, to a slack channel.
