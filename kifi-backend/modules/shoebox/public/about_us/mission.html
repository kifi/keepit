We’re an early stage startup, on a mission to improve people's lives at scale, looking for top tier engineers and innovative product designers.

The internet has not yet fully delivered on its promise.

Imagine a world where billions of people connect with the people, places and things that matter to them most, from any device… where the most important things are effortlessly organized, search is deeply personalized, and discovery is meaningful and delightful. It's a new world where knowledge is shared seamlessly and collaboratively, and serendipitous interest-based collisions are commonplace… fueled by people’s connections and genuine interests. At our core, we’re about people helping people, by simply helping themselves.

Check out kifi, where we’ve unveiled our first set of beta features. It's the best way to keep things organized and find the things that matter from your friends. It’s just the beginning of the first chapter in our story.

If you are passionate about creating this wonderful new world with our inspired group of creators, we want to hear from you.
