General Partner at billion-dollar venture capital firm Apax Partners; co-headed Silicon Valley office and Technology Practice, 1994–2007

About Oren:

Oren loves backing passionate founders building great companies, mostly in the internet and mobile internet spaces. Oren backed successful companies like Houzz, Audible, Chegg and others who are re-defining their respective industries. Oren backs two or three companies per year and makes significant investments in each.

Oren sees his role as working for the companies and the CEOs. He believes in good karma – his vast experience has helped him discover that the more he does for others, the more it returns to him in ways he could not have imagined.

Oren believes that his success as an investor, which is exceptional relative to any VC or angel benchmark, is a function of:
1. Luck
2. Focus on a limited number of companies
3. Always putting the interests of the portfolio companies first, ahead of any other consideration.
