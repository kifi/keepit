What is kifi

Kifi (short for keep it find it) allows you to easily keep and tag anything you find online – an article, video, picture, email, just anything – then quickly find it on top of your favorite search engine results, together with relevant pages that your friends kept, too.

kifi also lets you message your friends about what you keep and find alongside any webpage, to get their opinion or gain from their expertise. kifi even lets you highlight specific sections of the page and add them to your conversation with a simple 'look here' shortcut, helping you and your friends find those sections easily, in real-time.

Finally, kifi lets you browse and organize all your keeps and tags on kifi.com. This is also where you invite friends from your contact lists and social networks to join you on kifi.

The vision behind kifi

We built kifi to serve as an extension of your brain and the collective brain of the people you trust. Just imagine a world where people could help each other find what they were looking for by simply keeping the things they found valuable.

This is what we set out to do with kifi. It required the development of a new technology – the 'Find Engine' – which personalizes your search results based on who you are, who your friends are, and all your shared interests.

When using kifi, not only can you gain from the fruits of your past research, but you can gain from the time put in by your friends, as well. So instead of starting every search from scratch, you can build upon all the discoveries made by the people you trust the most.

It's a world where friends help each other simply by being themselves. Where discoveries are shared for the benefit of us all. One of greater connectivity, and deeper validation. It's the world of kifi. Welcome.
