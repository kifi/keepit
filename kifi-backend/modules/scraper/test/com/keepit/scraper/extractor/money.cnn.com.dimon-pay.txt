That's up from a $10 million bonus for 2012. His $1.5 million base salary remains unchanged. </p> <div id="ie_column"> <div id="quigo220"><!-- ADSPACE: business_news/quigo/ctr.220x200 -->
<div id="ad-c1d8b58b1d51203a" align="center" style="padding: 0; margin: 0; border: 0;">

</div>
<!-- ADSPACE-END --></div> </div> <p> The bank said its board considered "several key factors." Among them: JPMorgan's "sustained long-term performance" and its efforts to put its legal problems behind it. </p> <p> Dimon, CEO and chairman, had his total <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2013/01/16/investing/jpmorgan-earnings/'>2012 pay slashed</a> by about half to $11.5 million after JPMorgan suffered estimated trading loss of nearly $6 billion during the year attributed to a <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2013/03/14/investing/jpmorgan-senate/index.html'>London trader</a> known as the London Whale. </p> <p> During 2013 the bank agreed to pay $1 billion in fines to U.S. and UK regulators for <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2013/09/19/investing/jpmorgan-london-whale-fine/'>lack of proper oversight</a> of its traders related to that loss. </p> <p> But that was only a fraction the fines and legal expenses the bank shelled out during the year for a variety of transgressions. </p> <p> SNL Financial estimates that total payouts by the bank came to $18.6 billion for 2013 alone, as well as more than $1 billion in attorney costs during the year. </p> <p> Provisions for those payments forced the bank to report a <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2013/10/11/investing/jpmorgan-earnings/'>loss</a> in the third quarter, the first of Dimon's tenure as CEO. For the full year, the bank reported net income of $17.9 billion, down 16% from the previous year. But shares increased 33% during the year. </p> <p> Dimon had been the <a href='http://money.cnn.com/2012/06/20/news/companies/bank-ceo-pay/'>top paid bank CEO</a> in 2011 before the problems of the last two years. </p> <p> The decision to raise his pay was first reported by the <i>New York Times</i> ahead of the filing. <a href="#TOP"> <img width="7" height="7" border="0" alt="To top of page" src="http://i.cdn.turner.com/money/images/bug.gif"> </a> </p> <div id="storyFooter"></div> <br /><br /> <div class="storytimestamp"> First Published: January 24, 2014: 8:31 AM ET </div> </div> <!--/storytext--> <div class="foot"> <div id="postedin"></div> <div class="clearFloat"></div> </div> </div> <div id="quigo628"><!-- ADSPACE: business_news/quigo/bot.628x215 -->
